MORE TO LOVE

  • MASA allery Mexico City

    Disrupting the line between art and design, MASA Gallery opens in Mexico City with its first premanent home. Occupying 600m2 of gallery space in an 18th century colonial house, the nomadic gallery's exoansion launches with two solo exhibitions, from artist and designer Brian Thoreen, and multidisciplinary conceptual artist Mario Garcia Torres. 8 February - 8 April 2023

  • Beyond the Streets

    Bringing together the work of over 100 artists, from defiant train writers, to powerful large-scale muralists, the UK's biggest graffiti & street art exhibition comes to the Saatchi Gallery, featuring Charlie Ahearn, GOLDIE, Guerrilla Girls, Jenny Holzer and more in a showcase of defining moments in the movement's history. Supported by Adidas. Until 9 May 2023.

  • Hyper Functional, Ultra Healthy

    Somerset House Studios tackles wellness trends and the mythology of the 'good body' as the series returns. With a focus on disability justice, explored through newly commissioned artworks, films, workshops, and conversations, artists engage with the space of health and care, including new works from Christine Sun Kim, Jamila Prowse, bare minimum collective, and Djofray Makumbu. Until March 2023
